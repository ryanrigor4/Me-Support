Information

ME Support was created in inspiration to provide small resources to help people manage stress without leaving the comfort of their home! The creators behind the app came up with the name very carefully to make sure our users know what we are about. Our website provides many resources including being able to do a quick or super long meditation, completely tailored to you, using our built in timer. Alongside that we have our random quote generator which will display an inspirational quote to start your day or give you a boost anytime of the day! We hope you find some routine with this website and build your good habits that we all face with receiving mental health support, and it is important to know that you are not alone and we are here to help!
